## About me

- I recently joined Sichuan University. Previously, I was a Ph.D. student in the School of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science (EECS) at Washington State University, Pullman, advised by Prof. [Haipeng Cai](https://chapering.github.io/).
- I am interested in cross-language software and security. My research focuses on detecting and mitigating multilingual software bugs, analyzing cross-language vulnerabilities, and developing robust methodologies for ensuring software reliability in multi-language environments. 
- I explore dynamic and static analysis techniques, language-agnostic debugging, and security implications arising from multilingual code interactions. Through my work, I aim to enhance the understanding of cross-language software engineering challenges and contribute to more secure and efficient multi-language programming practices.
